How to Play ?


  1. 1. Start the Game: Click the Start button to begin.
  2. 2. Watch: The game will highlight a sequence of colors.
  3. 3. Repeat the Sequence: Click the buttons in the same order as they were highlighted.
  4. 4. Follow the Pattern: Keep repeating the growing sequence. The game gets harder with each round!!
  5. 5. Make a Mistake? If you click the wrong button, the game ends.
  6. 6. Play Again: Press Play Again to beat your previous high score!!
